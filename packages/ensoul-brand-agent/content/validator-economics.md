# Validator economics

A validator runs CometBFT consensus on a $5/month VPS. It earns block rewards proportional to its voting power, plus a share of storage fees from the agents it helps persist. Pioneer validators receive a 1M ENSL foundation delegation locked for 24 months.

---

Block rewards in year one: roughly 273,000 ENSL per day across the network. Per-validator earnings depend on stake. The Pioneer program funds the first 20 external validators with foundation delegation so they can begin earning from day one.
