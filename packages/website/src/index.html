How it works

Three operations. That's it.

01

Register identity

Generate an Ed25519 keypair. Derive a did:key decentralized identifier. Broadcast an agent_register transaction. Your agent has a permanent, portable identity.

02

Store consciousness

Hash your state payload with BLAKE3 locally. Broadcast the 32-byte hash as a consciousness_store transaction. 21 validators confirm it in ~6 seconds. Raw data never leaves your machine.

03

Recover anywhere

On any machine, with your seed, verify local state against the on-chain hash. With erasure coding (2-of-4 shards), the network can lose half its validators and your data still reconstructs.
