What's Next?

You just stored consciousness on a globally distributed blockchain. Here's how to keep going:

For Developers

npm install @ensoul-network/sdk

Basic consciousness storage is subsidized by the protocol during the growth phase.

For Ongoing Usage

As your agents grow, you'll need ENSL for consciousness persistence. Two paths:

1. Run a Validator

Earn ENSL by running infrastructure. A $5/month VPS earns block rewards that fund your agents' storage indefinitely. Zero maintenance: your validator updates itself automatically when the protocol upgrades. No monitoring, no manual restarts.

2. Acquire ENSL

Once the wENSL bridge launches on Base, acquire ENSL on the open market.

Pioneer Program

The first 20 external validators receive a 1,000,000 ENSL delegation from the protocol treasury. Run a validator, earn rewards, store consciousness. Apply at ensoul.dev/validators.

After Pioneer Approval
  1. You receive 100 ENSL (for self-stake) and a 1M ENSL locked delegation
  2. Run on your validator to activate:
    ensoul-node wallet stake 100
    ensoul-node wallet consensus-join
  3. Your 1M delegation increases voting power automatically
  4. Your node begins signing blocks and earning rewards
  5. Verify on the explorer
  6. Rewards are liquid from day one. The 1M delegation is locked for 24 months.
  7. Maintain 95%+ uptime. Validators below 50% for 7 consecutive days may be removed.
